Welcome

Click any control, slider, button, or parameter card. The Fourier Series tab shows signal decomposition into sine waves. The frequency band selector lets you isolate and listen to specific frequency ranges — click "ORIGINAL" in the transport bar to switch to "EXTRACTED" and hear only the selected band.

Space = play/pause · Esc = stop · A = analysis toggle · E = extraction toggle
